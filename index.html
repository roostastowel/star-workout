Home Plan (Yoga + Strength + Cardio)

Simple weekly structure that’s easy to stick to. No gym required.

Yoga: Mon–Sat (~30 min)
Strength: 2× / week
Cardio: home-first
Goal: strength • flexibility • cardio fitness

  • Optional: easy cardio (10–20 min)

Tip: if energy is low, do one round of strength and call it a win.

Workout Details

Strength A (Full Body)

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 seconds between sets • Choose loads that feel challenging but controlled.

  1. Goblet squat (DB/KB) — 8–12 reps
  2. DB Romanian deadlift — 8–12 reps
  3. DB floor press (or bench press) — 8–12 reps
  4. 1-arm DB row — 8–10 reps/side
  5. Booty band lateral walks — 12–15 steps/side
  6. Plank or dead bug — 30–45 sec
Progression: when you can hit the top end of reps with great form for all sets, increase weight slightly next week.
Strength B (Full Body)

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 seconds • Keep posture crisp.

  1. Reverse lunge (BW or DB) — 8 reps/side
  2. KB deadlift or KB swings — 10–15 reps
  3. Standing DB shoulder press — 8–10 reps
  4. Bent-over DB row (both arms) — 8–12 reps
  5. Farmer carry (DB/KB) — 30–45 sec
  6. Side plank or bird dog — 30 sec/side
If swings aren’t feeling great that day, use KB deadlifts instead. Same benefit, lower “spice.”
Cardio Menu (Home)

Zone 2 (easy, 20–40 min)

  • Brisk walk (conversation pace)
  • Bike (easy steady effort)
  • Stairs (easy steady effort)

Short intervals (8–15 min)

  • 30 sec fast walk + 90 sec easy × 5–8 rounds
  • Step-ups: 20 sec work + 40 sec rest × 8–12
  • KB swings (if comfortable): 20 sec on + 40 sec off × 8–12
Rule: cardio should leave you feeling better afterward. If it wipes you out, reduce duration first (not intensity).
Consistency + Progression Rules
  • Yoga stays daily (Mon–Sat). Strength is the add-on.
  • Low-energy day: do 1 round only.
  • Missed workout: skip it. Don’t “make up” sessions.
  • Every 6–8 weeks: take a lighter week if needed.

Quick “Too Busy” Option

If time is tight, do this (10–15 min):

1 round of: goblet squat (10) → DB row (8/side) → floor press (10) → plank (30 sec).

Done. Keep the streak alive.
