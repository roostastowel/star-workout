Weekly Strength • Flexibility • Cardio Plan

Focus: Strength, flexibility, cardiopulmonary fitness
Equipment: Dumbbells, kettlebells, booty band, bodyweight
Yoga: Yoga with Adriene — Monday through Saturday (~30 min)

Weekly Overview

Monday
Yoga + Strength A + optional short cardio
Tuesday
Yoga only (evening work day)
Wednesday
Yoga + optional easy cardio
Thursday
Yoga + Strength B + short cardio
Friday
Yoga only
Saturday
Yoga + optional fun movement
Sunday
Rest or gentle walk

Strength A – Full Body

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 sec

• Goblet squat — 8–12 reps
• Dumbbell Romanian deadlift — 8–12 reps
• Dumbbell floor or bench press — 8–12 reps
• One-arm dumbbell row — 8–10 / side
• Booty band lateral walks — 12–15 steps / side
• Plank or dead bug — 30–45 sec
Progress when reps feel strong and controlled.

Strength B – Full Body

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 sec

• Reverse lunge — 8 reps / side
• Kettlebell deadlift or swings — 10–15 reps
• Standing DB shoulder press — 8–10 reps
• Bent-over DB row — 8–12 reps
• Farmer carries — 30–45 sec
• Side plank or bird dog — 30 sec / side
Posture over weight.

Cardio Options (Home)

Easy / Zone 2

  • Brisk walking
  • Stationary bike
  • Easy stairs

Short Intervals

  • 30s fast walk / 90s easy × 5–8
  • KB swings or step-ups: 20s on / 40s off
Cardio should feel energizing.

Consistency Rules

  • Yoga stays daily
  • Low energy = fewer rounds
  • No “make-up” workouts
  • Consistency beats intensity

Progression

  • Add weight slowly
  • Add a round after 3–4 weeks
  • Deload every 6–8 weeks
