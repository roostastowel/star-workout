Home Plan (Yoga + Strength + Cardio)

Simple weekly structure that’s easy to stick to. No gym required.

Yoga: Mon–Sat (~30 min)
Strength: 2× / week
Cardio: home-first
Goal: strength • flexibility • cardio fitness

Workout Details

Strength A (Full Body)

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 seconds between sets • Choose loads that feel challenging but controlled.

  1. Goblet squat (DB/KB) — 8–12 reps
  2. DB Romanian deadlift — 8–12 reps
  3. DB floor press (or bench press) — 8–12 reps
  4. 1-arm DB row — 8–10 reps/side
  5. Booty band lateral walks — 12–15 steps/side
  6. Plank or dead bug — 30–45 sec
Progression: when you can hit the top end of reps with great form for all sets, increase weight slightly next week.
Strength B (Full Body)

2–3 rounds • Rest ~60–90 seconds • Keep posture crisp.

  1. Reverse lunge (BW or DB) — 8 reps/side
  2. KB deadlift or KB swings — 10–15 reps
  3. Standing DB shoulder press — 8–10 reps
  4. Bent-over DB row (both
